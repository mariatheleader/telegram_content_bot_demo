How We Work Together

4 months.
One plan that's completely yours.

Month 1

Intake and Discovery

Maria interviews you inside Telegram to get to know your child — their interests, goals, schedule, and non-negotiables. You complete the REASSURED Observational Assessment so Maria understands your child's developmental baseline. This is not a test. You simply share what you know about your child.

Month 2

Curriculum Shopping and Building

Maria presents curriculum recommendations based on your child's assessment results. You go back and forth on materials — Amazon picks, supplements, things you've been eyeing. You decide. Maria organizes everything into a daily, weekly, and monthly structure.

Month 3

Building Your Routine and Refinements

Maria ensures your curriculum and material stack covers your state's homeschool requirements, adds what's needed, and removes what doesn't serve your family. You refine together until it feels completely yours.

Month 4

Organized Routine Delivered

You receive your complete organized digital plan — ready to print and post on your refrigerator. Everything is planned for the year. Your children's curriculum, your choices, your routine. You have peace of mind because you are prepared.
